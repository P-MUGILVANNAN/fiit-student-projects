About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Savory Bites has been serving authentic cuisine with a modern twist. Our chef, with over 20 years of experience in Michelin-starred restaurants around the world, brings you a unique dining experience.

We source only the freshest ingredients from local farmers and suppliers to ensure the highest quality in every dish we serve.

Our Chef

Chef Marco Bellini

Executive Chef

With over 25 years of culinary experience, Chef Marco brings his expertise from Michelin-starred restaurants in Italy, France, and Japan to Savory Bites.

His philosophy revolves around using the freshest seasonal ingredients to create dishes that are both innovative and rooted in tradition.

"Cooking is not just about feeding people, it's about creating memorable experiences," says Chef Marco.
